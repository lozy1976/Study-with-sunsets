The photos above were taken by a friend that used to work as an underwater photographer. It's only a small sample of the beautiful sea life here, but I will be making updates to my site regularly, and hope to be able to add some more soon.

Cozumel is extremely popular with scuba divers and people that love the sea. At the time of writing this I haven't learnt to dive, but it's in my plans for the near future. I've only tried snorkeling, but even that completely blew me away. The transparency of the water means that you can still see amazing sea life on the bottom when you snorkel at 30 feet. Unfortunately, I experienced a very scary moment in big waves in Costa Rica some years back, which has affected my confidence in the water. However, I've made a lot of progress since being here and I absolutely adore the water.

There are many activities in the water that you can try when you're here including: snorkeling, scuba diving, kitesurfing, surfing and parasailing.
